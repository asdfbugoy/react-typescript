Wireless Access Point

Your current solution includes a total of X wireless access points for your site with Y floors Add on up to Z wireless access points to increase your Wifi coverage.
Each Wireless Access Point comes with onsite installation, 100m of cabling for connection between Access Point and POE Switch, 24x7 active monitoring and Change Management to adjust the configurations of your access points.
We recommend the following based on the best practices.
  • Meeting Rooms: one access point for each meeting room.
  • Large Open Spaces: one access point per 100 square meter.

Security Camera

Improve physical security and enjoy useful analytics by adding on up to Z Security Camera with local storage to your SD-LAN network.
Each Security Camera comes with onsite installation with 100m of cabling for connection of Camera to your POE Switch, 24x7 active monitoring and change management to adjust the settings of your cameras.
All video footages are stored locally in the camera only, allowing the camera to be placed in your network without significant increase in bandwidth usage. Our security camera comes with video analytics, from motion detection to heat maps.
Minimize the number of Security Cameras required with our wide viewing angle (Horizontal 114 & Vertical 61 degrees) .
Note: Video footage is non real time when end user access the video footage through wifi connection or external network.
